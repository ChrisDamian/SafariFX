### Notes

1. All charges are exclusive of applicable Kenyan taxes (Excise Duty at 20% on financial transaction fees, effective January 2026).
2. Correspondent bank charges may vary based on intermediary routing and beneficiary bank location.
3. **SHA** (shared) means sender and beneficiary each pay their own bank's charges. **OUR** means sender pays all charges including correspondent and beneficiary bank fees.
4. Negotiated/preferential rates are available through respective bank treasury desks and are subject to prevailing market conditions and CBK guidelines.
5. All rates and charges are subject to change without prior notice, in accordance with CBK Prudential Guidelines.
